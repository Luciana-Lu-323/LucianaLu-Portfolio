National Teaching-Aid Creative Competition

2021 · STEM · Geometry · Merit Award (youngest)

I built a 3D icosahedron model inspired by COVID-19 visuals, to explore why viruses often appear spherical. The piece was exhibited at the national teaching-aid competition and received a Merit Award as the youngest participant.

  • Focus: Polyhedra, minimal surface, stability
  • Skills: Research, modeling, public presentation
  • Solo entry: independent presentation and Q&A

Exhibit & Proof

I was selected to exhibit at the national event and presented the model to a panel of judges. The work was evaluated on scientific rigor, clarity, and craftsmanship, and I received a Merit Award as one of the youngest participants.

我入選全國決選並於現場向評審口頭簡報與答詢，作品依科學性、表達與製作完成度進行評分， 最終獲得甲等／佳作獎項（同場年紀最小的得獎者之一）。
