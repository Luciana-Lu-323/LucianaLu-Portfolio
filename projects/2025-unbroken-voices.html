Unbroken Voices — Anti-Bullying Project

2025 · Media · Advocacy · Youth Voice

After experiencing bullying, I started Unbroken Voices in 2025 to amplify students’ stories and provide practical resources for wellbeing and action. The project includes interviews, short-form videos, and a resource kit designed with school counselors and peers.

  • Format: IG Reels / YouTube Shorts / Long-form blog posts
  • Outcome: Awareness, reporting guidance, peer-support scripts
  • Next: School workshop kit & partner counselor network
